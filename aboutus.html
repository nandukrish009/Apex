ABOUT US



APEX Precision Technologies is established in JAN-2014, having a high tech toolroom facility at Thiruvallur (Near Chennai city) in India & started it’s operations to supply the global tooling requirements in competitive price with high quality and commitments.The company is promoted by well experienced Engineers, who are having good working knowledge in high precision Moulds & Tools manufacturing industries over 15 years.Our dedicated engineers who work with customers requirement in manufacturing of precision moulds & tools parts,precision engineering components, precision gauges, jigs & fixtures for Automotive, Electrical & Electronics (especially for Semiconductor, Connector industries) Medical, Aerospace & other applications, and also ensuring with satisfactory results.We are specialized in machining of carbide (Blanks & Pins), Steels, Aluminum, Brass, Copper, and also Plastic materials of Delrin, Peek, PVC, Acrylic etc.
